Time Critical (NFO & OBC)

[ NFO ]

Spread Courier has contracted both commercial and private air carriers to serve more than 140 countries and territories.

We select fastest routings in accordance with airlines performance, status of weather, airport operation capability and aircraft’s security.

All import and export documents will be pre-audited ahead of any movements to ensure the shortest clearance and declaration processes. To East Europe especially, Spread Courier is proud to announce that a delivery can be done in 30hours!

[ Hand Carry ]

Spread Courier provides second-to-none secured and accountable Hand Carry services global wise.  All our couriers are highly trained to secure and expedite your shipments. All the way dedicated handling and cost saving delivery are Spread Courier’s Number One Goal ALWAYS.
