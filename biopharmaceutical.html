Biopharmaceutical / Clinical Trial

SpreadCourier is the most reliable biopharmaceutical courier in Asia; the company is formed by several professionals who have tremendous experience in serving the bioscience and major global express industries. We have handled numerous biological logistics on clinical trials, human blood, /tissue/plasma/bone marrow…and live animals; we have covered many countries including USA, Europe, Latin America, Middle East and Asia.

Accumulating 30 years experience, SpreadCourier's biopharmaceutical team is committed to serve pharmaceutical companies, CRO and labs worldwide. We offer our logistics expertise on transporting biological materials such as infectious (UN2814), noninfectious (UN3373)…., ambient and temperature controlled shipments (2-8 degree, 15-25 degree, frozen).

To assist our clients with total solutions on handling biological materials, SpreadCourier continuously strengthen our full range services to meet your needs. These include:

  • Consultation on import and export applications in conforming regulations of FDA, DOH, SFDA, etc.
  • Certification of Thermo Boxes, Dry Ice, Gel Pack...
  • Cold chain storage

We are here to serve you every day and every hour of a year without any exceptions!
