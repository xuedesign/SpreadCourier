Dangerous Goods (DG)

Spread Courier are staffed with well trained personnel, who are certified in International Air Transport Association (IATA) Dangerous Goods (DG) Regulations.

As Spread Courier DG team’s customers, you will enjoy

  • On-site experts who are available 24/7/365 to review documentation and ensure accuracy before tendering to a carrier.
  • Assistance with understanding regulations and staying current with classification changes to the regulation.
  • Ensure that your packaging, DG labels or markings, and Shippers Declaration comply with the most current IATA DG Regulations.
  • Assistance with preparing and organizing DG documentation for shipping.
