About us

SpreadCourier is Asia’s premier logistics provider specializing in NFO, AOG, Biopharm and cold chain transport. We leverage our best in class employees, relationship and worldwide network to ensure that your critical shipment is delivered on time and every time.

We are Greater China only provider of qualified temperature controlled shipping solution. We have in country stock to ensure that your sensitive items are protected from excursions.

Why choose Spreadcourier

  • Over 15 years servicing Biopharm, Automotive and aerospace customers in Asia.
  • Spread Networks are all qualified.
  • Spreadcourier best in worldwide network of preferred airlines, vendors and Asia airline partners.
  • USE more of competitive and transports rates catered to your specific needs.
  • Variety of active and passive temperature controlled shipping solutions for your import and export needs.
  • Logistics regulatory and compliance experts in the APAC region. Let us help you navigate the China market.
